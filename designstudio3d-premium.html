Premium · Photoreal 3D

Your real room, in true 3D

Film a slow 20–60 second walk around your room and we build a photorealistic 3D copy of it — not a sketch, the actual room: your furniture, your light, your floors. Step inside it in the browser and walk through it from any angle. It's the same Gaussian-splatting technology used in high-end real-estate capture.

Looking for the free editable planner instead? That's our Design Studio 3D — it sketches your room with AI so you can drag furniture around and restyle it. This Premium studio is about a true-to-life walkthrough of the space as it is today.

🎥 Filming tips: move slowly and smoothly in a circle around the room, keep good lighting on, and let each view overlap the last. 1080p is plenty. You can also pick 20+ photos taken all around the room instead of a video.
How it works
1
Film your room
A slow 20–60s walk-around on your phone. The video itself never leaves your device — we only read ~24 still frames from it in your browser.
2
We build it in 3D
Cloud processing takes ~7–20 minutes. Leave the page and come back anytime — your scan keeps going and this page picks it right up.
3
Step inside
Walk through the photoreal copy of your room in the browser, share it with our team, and plan the renovation around the real space.
Beta: scans are built from 24 frames, so results are lifelike but slightly soft. A failed scan usually means the walk was too fast or the room too dark — just film again.
