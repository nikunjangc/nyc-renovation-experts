Upload a photo of your kitchen or bathroom. Our AI identifies every fixture and appliance, then helps you find real replacement products from Home Depot, Lowe's, IKEA, Amazon & more — with a 3D preview of how the new piece will look.

1. Upload a photo
Drag & drop or tap to upload

JPG or PNG, up to 10MB. Best results: well-lit, head-on view of the room.

2. Detected fixtures
Click the photo where the fixture is.
Click a chip above (or directly on the photo) to find replacement products.
3. A few quick choices AI

Picking your preferences makes the product matches much more accurate.

4. Replacement products
Real prices from Home Depot, Lowe's, IKEA, Amazon & more. Click any product to preview it in 3D.
5. Drop it in your room Beta

The 3D model is placed where your fixture was. Drag to reposition, scroll / pinch on the model to resize, right-click + drag (or two-finger drag) on the model to rotate it.

Rendering 3D model…
~20-40 seconds with Trellis.
2D mode: drag the orange box to where the product should go, then click Render in my room — the AI only edits inside that box and preserves the rest of your photo exactly. 3D mode: opt-in — generates a rotatable 3D model when toggled (~20-40s, separate cost).
